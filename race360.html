About RACE360

In a constantly changing universe where pace seems to the norm, now more than ever, technology has the potential to revolutionize the world as we experience it. The technological breakthroughs of the recent past have set the foundation for a future world with an array of phenomenal opportunities and possibilities. The challenge ahead of us is to find ways and means to harness these technologies to turn this potential into reality. RACE360, organized by REVA Academy for Corporate Excellence (RACE), an initiative of REVA University is a confluence of industry and academia thought leaders to brain storm on technology landscape, emerging trends and futuristic organizations. This Emerging Technology Conference explores the technologies of tomorrow that are shaping the world and human race at large. This conference endeavours to bring perspectives on how cutting-edge technologies like Cognitive Computing, Artificial Intelligence, Blockchain, Cybersecurity, Augmented Reality, Edge Computing, and last but not the least, a connected world will create a better future for humanity.

Who should attend?

  • Emerging Technology Professionals and Enthusiasts
  • Cybersecurity Professionals and Aspirants
  • Data Scientists, Analysts and Engineers
  • Innovators, Entrepreneurs and Thought Leaders
  • Anyone else who has interest in the broad technology industry

Why attend?

  • Connect with thought leaders, entrepreneurs, fellow professionals and technology aspirants
  • Deepen your understanding of technologies
  • Learn about the new innovations in emerging technologies
  • Attend workshops for hands-on experience in Artificial Intelligence, Cybersecurity and Blockchain
  • Be part of the Hackathons and win exciting prizes
